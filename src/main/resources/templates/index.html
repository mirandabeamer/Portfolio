About Me

Hello! I am a full stack Java Developer currently seeking a new opportunity.

My interest in programming began while working as a dietitian in the hospital. Using a variety of software programs per day, some great and some not so much, it became apparent how much technology can affect productivity, patient care, and even morale. I could see where various areas of applications needed to be improved and decided I wanted to be a part of the team that could do so. I began taking free online tutorials at home and developed a passion for coding, eventually enrolling in The Software Guild.

A year later, I am a graduate of the program and have built multiple web applications using a variety of technologies and now call myself a full stack developer. I am already working on how I can improve the field of dietetics using what I've learned by building a web application with quick calculators and tools for dietitians to relieve stress and human error.

My career goal is to be continuously learning, improving, and growing in the field. I am hoping to find a team with whom I can do so with. Please take a look at what I've been coding lately and feel free to reach out via email or LinkedIn!
